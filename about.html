About RDF/SPARQL Starter Kit

The starter kit is very low-level, intended to help developers get started with other tasks like creating APIs functions based on the graph contents.

The starter kit makes it easy to connect to a SPARQL endpoint; configure a graph and namespaces; and insert/delete triples.

A viewer is available for browsing the "triples" in the graph.

The starter kit is NOT meant to "visualize" RDF graphs. Again, this is very low level for developers.
